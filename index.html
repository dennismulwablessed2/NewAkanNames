Akan names

Naming Culture in Ghana is mainly based on the days of the week

Below is how the Akan name their male and female according to the days of the week

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednessday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednessday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
